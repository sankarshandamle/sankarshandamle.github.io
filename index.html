About Me

I am a post-doctoral researcher at the Artificial Intelligence Laboratory (LIA), EPFL, under Prof. Boi Faltings.

Before that, I did my Ph.D. in Computer Science at Machine Learning Lab, IIIT, Hyderabad, under Dr. Sujit Gujar. My Ph.D. was fully funded through the Ripple-IIITH Fellowship. I completed my Bachelor of Technology (w/ Honors) in Computer Science, also at IIIT, Hyderabad.

My research focuses on designing fair, private, and strategy-proof mechanisms for multi-agent systems, combining insights from machine learning, game theory, and privacy. I have collaborated with researchers from premier institutions such as IIIT Hyderabad, EPFL and HKUST, as well as industry labs including Microsoft Research India and Samsung Research India, working on real-world applications that require trustworthy and robust AI solutions.
